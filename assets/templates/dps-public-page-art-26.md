## 2. Available switching and porting procedures

For each service we offer, customers may switch by:

- (a) initiating a switch via [INSERT — portal URL or contact path];
- (b) requesting an export of exportable data, returned in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format;
- (c) where applicable, executing a switch via the open interface(s) listed in section 4 below.

The end-to-end procedure is:

1. Customer initiates switching by notifying [INSERT — Provider contact / portal] in accordance with the customer's contract.
2. The notice period of up to 2 months commences (per Article 25(2)(d)).
3. At the end of the notice period, the 30-day mandatory transitional period commences (per Article 25(2)(a)). During this period, the contract remains in force; we provide assistance to the customer and any authorised destination provider; we maintain business continuity; we provide information on known risks; and we maintain a high level of security.
4. After the transitional period, a retrieval period of at least 30 calendar days runs (per Article 25(2)(g)).
5. Erasure follows successful switching (per Article 25(2)(h)).

The customer may extend the transitional period once (Article 25(5)). The Provider may propose an alternative transitional period (≤ 7 months) where 30 days is technically infeasible, justified in writing within 14 working days of the switching request (Article 25(4)).
