**Drafting notes (for the lawyer; remove before dispatch)**

- Verify the third-party recipient is not a designated gatekeeper at the time of the request — check the current Commission DMA list.
- The scope of the request should be specific. A request for "all data" is likely to provoke push-back and may delay response.
- Where personal data is involved, ensure a GDPR analysis is in place before dispatch.
- If the User wishes to receive the data themselves and onward share, that is a separate Article 4 access request, not an Article 5 third-party transfer.
- Section 8 verification should be proportionate. For an established account, no additional verification beyond authenticated channels should typically be necessary.
