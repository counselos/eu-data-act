**Drafting notes (for the lawyer; remove before issuance)**

- Verify that all four Article 3(2) items (a)–(d) are populated. A pre-contract notice missing any of these items is non-compliant.
- Verify the volume estimates are realistic and capable of being objectively justified.
- Where the Connected Product is also paired with a related service, ensure a corresponding Article 3(3) notice is prepared.
- For products placed on the market after 12 September 2026, the Article 3(1) "by design" obligation also applies — direct access where relevant and technically feasible.
- Confirm whether a sectoral overlay applies (automotive type-approval, MDR, NIS2, etc.); if so, the sectoral overlay may impose additional or different disclosure requirements.
- The Data Holder's contact in section 7 must enable quick and efficient communication, per Art. 3(3)(e). Apply the same standard here even though Art. 3(2) does not expressly require it — this is best practice.
