**Drafting notes (for the lawyer; remove before dispatch)**

- This notice is sent under Article 32(5) **before complying** with the request, except where the request serves law-enforcement purposes and notification would defeat that purpose. Verify the law-enforcement carve-out before assuming notice is required.
- The Article 32(2)/(3) assessment must be on objective elements. Refer to the Provider's general measures statement under Article 28 for context.
- Verify that the data sought is **non-personal data**. For personal data, GDPR Chapter V (international transfers) governs separately; this notice would need to address that as well.
- For US CLOUD Act-style requests, the lawyer must verify how Article 32 applies in the specific corporate structure and any indemnity / cooperation obligations under the customer contract.
- The customer's options in section 4 should match what is technically and contractually feasible; do not list options that are not available.
