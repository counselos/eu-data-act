Pursuant to Article 25 of Regulation (EU) 2023/2854 (the "**Data Act**") and the switching clauses in our contract dated [INSERT — contract date], we hereby give notice of our decision to switch from the data processing service identified below.

## 1. Service to which this notice relates

## 2. Notice period

This notice begins the contractual notice period of up to two (2) months under Article 25(2)(d) and the corresponding clause in our contract.

## 3. Choice at end of notice (Article 25(3))

We hereby notify the Provider of our intended action upon termination of the notice period. We have selected option **[a / b / c]** below:

- (a) **Switch to a different DPS provider.** Destination provider details:
  - Name: [INSERT]
  - Address: [INSERT]
  - Contact: [INSERT — name, email, phone]
- (b) **Switch to an on-premises ICT infrastructure.** [INSERT — confirm intent to receive exportable data and digital assets to our on-premises infrastructure.]
- (c) **Erase exportable data and digital assets.** [INSERT — confirm intent to terminate the contract and require full erasure under Article 25(2)(h).]

## 4. Customer's right to extend the transitional period

The Customer reserves its right under Article 25(5) to extend the transitional period once for a period the Customer considers more appropriate, by further written notice.

## 5. Provider's procedure

We expect the Provider to:

- (a) provide reasonable assistance to us and to the destination provider in the switching process during the 30-day mandatory transitional period (Article 25(2)(a));
- (b) act with due care to maintain business continuity and continue the provision of functions or services under the contract;
- (c) provide clear information concerning known risks to continuity;
- (d) maintain a high level of security throughout the switching process, including during the retrieval period; and
- (e) where the 30-day transitional period is technically infeasible, notify us within 14 working days, justify in writing, and propose an alternative transitional period not exceeding 7 months (Article 25(4)).

## 6. Categories of data and digital assets to be ported

Per the schedule attached to our contract, the categories of data and digital assets to be ported include all exportable data within the meaning of Article 2(38) of the Data Act and the further categories listed in [INSERT — schedule reference]. Please confirm by [INSERT — date] that all such categories are included in the switching plan.

## 7. Format and standards

We expect data to be exported in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format. Where harmonised interoperability standards published in the central Union standards repository apply (Article 30(3)), please confirm compatibility. Where no such standards apply, please confirm export per Article 30(5).
